Evaluation Summary

You are scheduled to complete a midterm and final exam during this course. They must be invigilated at an approved site. Approved sites would be any of the Saskatchewan Polytechnic Testing Centres, any of the Regional Colleges, and may be Elementary or Secondary Schools. Other sites may be used providing they are pre-approved by the Instructor. You will be given a 1 week window of opportunity to arrange for and complete the Block Exam. Your course Instructor will provide additional details.
